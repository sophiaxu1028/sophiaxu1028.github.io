How to learn English?

Posted on 2019-6-23

Learning English in a different way?

After so many years learning English for taking exams, I felt my English ability is still very far from locals. Although some people around me think my English is pretty good, I still feel I cannot express myself freely in English. I love travelling, but how can I enjoy the trip fully without a good ability of expressing English. So, I decided to get a very huge improvment of my English in this year.

There are several tips recommended by Zhuzi here.

  1. brain soaking
  • bbc iplayer
  • Channel 4
  • youtube channels
  • podcast: Ted talks, Planet Money
    Find interesting materials first, and soak your brain in TVs everyday.
  1. read english only
    Try to read Tech websites more carefully. Read Twitter, Quora and Reddit every morning in the subway.
  1. focus on content first
    Understand the content and understand the language then.
  1. learn high frequency words
    Conclude some words that used a lot while watching tv shows.
    eg. stingy, trauma
  1. talk to yourself - shower talk
  1. record your readings
  1. find a native speaker to practice with
  1. accents (Newyork or LA accents)
    The last thing should pay attention to. Soak again.

Today is June 23. I will report my progress and feelings every week here. Go Sophia!
